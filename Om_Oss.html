About us

Organization

Pen and Paper is a voluntary, member-run rights organization that is religiously and politically neutral. We are based on individual membership. We are a nationwide organization where the members can be organized with pen and paper groups or be a member of the independent organization press children to education.

Why PenAndPaper? or our propose

According to UNICEF and the International Labor Organization 168 million children aged 5 to 17 are victims of child labour. Even though it can be argued that families who permit their children to work as opposed to encouraging them to attend school are irresponsible and immoral. However, for some families they have no choice other than allowing their children to participate in the child labour activates in order to live and survive and contribute financially.
Our goel is even the Child labour is not inevitable but it can be prevented or minimized to a low level through a number of elements, everyone has the right to education therefore freeing education for children at least in the elementary and fundamental stages, making elementary education compulsory. Our approach

We work particularly in remote and marginalized communities for the most part in developing’s country to get children presence at school through weekly investigation of every one’s absences. Our sustainable approach is changing the face of elementary and primary schools, even family’s perspectives in some areas of our continent.

Mission

our mission is to provide underprivileged children with opportunity to escape poverty. Education is a vital component of any effective effort to eradicate child labor, which is also a fundamental driver of global development, personal and national. However, millions of children around the world are deprived from their childhood, education, their potential and health this leading to a lifetime of poverty and social exclusion.
